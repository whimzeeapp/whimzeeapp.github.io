Privacy Policy

 

Overview


This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the Whimzee iPhone app.

 

We Collect No Personal Information Using Our Application


We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in Whimzee.


Individual settings relating to the Whimzee app are not personal and are stored only on your device.

 

App Analytics


To help us better understand how people use the Whimzee app and to continue improving them, we use analytics information provided by App Store Connect. This service provides us with some generic usage, transaction, and other statistics that are completely anonymous and cannot personally identify you in any way.

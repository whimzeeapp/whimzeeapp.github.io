FAQ

  1. Q: "How do I play?"
    A: Enter the names of the people who will be playing, choose either a number of rounds or time limit for the game, and then start the game! The game will tell you whose turn it is, and give that player a challenge with instructions about what to do next. Have fun playing!

  2. Q: "Will you be adding more challenges in the future?"
    A: That's the plan! How frequently depends on feedback from the app's users (that's you all!)


To report an issue, contact whimzeeapp@yahoo.com, and we will do our best to reply.
